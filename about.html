Our Story

Family-inspired care rooted in 15 years of experience, now serving the Chicago community.

Our Beginning

Born from Family Experience

VitalTouch Home Care was founded in 2024, but our roots in caregiving run much deeper. For over 15 years, our founder helped build and operate a successful home care agency alongside family, gaining invaluable experience serving seniors across Cook County and Chicago.

That experience taught us something important: elderly individuals deserve more than just assistance—they deserve attention, love, and respect. They deserve caregivers who see them as people, not patients.

VitalTouch was created to expand that mission. We are here to serve more seniors in our community with the same dedication and compassion that guided us for 15 years.

Our Approach

What Makes VitalTouch Different

We are not just another home care agency. Our founder comes from a technical background, and we have built VitalTouch with efficiency and innovation at its core.

Technology-Enhanced Care

Digital care logs, GPS-verified visits, and a family portal keep everyone connected and informed in real-time.

Family Caregiver Program

We help qualified family members become certified, paid caregivers through Medicaid. Keep care in the family while getting compensated.

Homemakers AND CNAs

We offer both homemaker services and certified nursing assistant care—whatever level of support your loved one needs.

Optional Smart Monitoring

For families who want extra peace of mind, we offer wearable fall detection, smart home sensors, and emergency response add-ons.
